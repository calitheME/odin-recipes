Lemon Pepper Tuna


Recipe by Alpha



Photo of lemon pepper tuna salad from Alpha.
Some rights reserved: Creative Commoms


Ingredients



  • 1 can tuna
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 shaker of lemon pepper seasoning


Directions



  1. Open and drain tuna can and pour tuna in bowl.

  2. Drizzle olive oil on tuna.

  3. Season with your heart and mix altogether.

  4. Serve with crackers and enjoy.
